Small Solar, Big Benefits
for Landowners

WynneWorks Energy works with landowners to install small solar projects and support the local electric grid.

When you partner with us, you have the potential to benefit from lease payments, solar installation at no charge to you, and a more dependable source of clean energy.

↓

Landowner Benefits

Hosting solar on your property can mean an additional revenue stream to diversify income.

Your Land, Your Terms

We tailor each project to respect your property and offer to remove installed equipment from the land to enable it to be returned to its prior use at the end of the lease term.

Predictable Revenue Stream

Once the site is permitted and the utility agrees to buy energy produced by the system, you can benefit from a stable, long-term revenue stream that’s not tied to crop yields or commodity markets.

Invest in Your Family's Future

Lease payments grow as the project moves from evaluation to operation, supporting today’s financial needs while keeping the land in your hands—helping you to retain the option to pass land down for generations.

No Upfront Cost or Tax Increases Due to Solar

We fund all project costs and handle solar maintenance, while you keep ownership and control of your property. The lease protects the owner from incremental taxes due to solar improvements.

A Wynne-ing Proposition™

  • Stable lease income: Receive long-term, contracted payments tied to project milestones and ongoing operations.
  • Landowner-first design: Site layout, access, and protections are tailored to how you use your land.
  • Local benefits: A stronger, more reliable grid and community tax revenue from clean power close to home.
  • Stewardship: Opportunities to choose pollinator habitat or another low-impact ground cover beneath ground-mount panels; the land remains “banked” for future uses compatible with system operations.
  • No upfront cost: We cover everything, including studies, permitting, interconnection, construction, operations, and maintenance.
